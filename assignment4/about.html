Welcome to Jarrell's Soul Food Restaurant, where we invite you to experience the warmth, flavors, and culture of Southern cuisine. Nestled in the heart of the vibrant city of Atlanta, our restaurant has been a beloved institution for over three decades, serving up soulful dishes that have become a cornerstone of the local dining scene. At Jarrell's, we take pride in preserving the rich traditions of Southern cooking while infusing our own unique twist to create a dining experience that is as memorable as it is delicious.

Our Story

Jarrell's Soul Food Restaurant has its roots in a long-standing family tradition of love for food and community. It all began with Grandma Martha, whose culinary expertise and warm hospitality were legendary in our family. Growing up, we fondly remember the enticing aromas that wafted from her kitchen, the sizzle of fried chicken, the comforting embrace of collard greens, and the mouthwatering sight of her famous peach cobbler. These cherished moments and flavors are etched into our memories, and they are the heart and soul of our restaurant.

In 1988, our family decided to share Grandma Martha's culinary legacy with the world by opening Jarrell's Soul Food Restaurant. Our aim was simple yet profound: to serve up authentic Southern soul food that would not only tantalize taste buds but also warm the hearts of all who walked through our doors. We named the restaurant after our youngest brother, Jarrell, as a tribute to the love and camaraderie that defined our family gatherings.

Over the years, Jarrell's has become more than just a restaurant; it has become a symbol of Southern hospitality, a place where friends and strangers alike come together to celebrate the joys of good food and good company. We've had the privilege of watching generations of families create their own cherished memories within our walls, just as we did growing up in Grandma Martha's kitchen.
